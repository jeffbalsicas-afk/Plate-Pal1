# Client-Side Demo Talk Script

## Introduction (30 seconds)
"Hello! Today I'll be demonstrating the client-side features of our catering booking platform. This system connects clients with professional caterers for their events. Let's dive in."

---

## Landing Page (1 minute)
"Here's our landing page. At the top, we have our navigation - Home, Browse Caterers, How It Works, and For Caterers. On the right, there's a Sign In button for existing users.

Below, you'll see our featured caterers section showcasing approved and active caterers on the platform. Each card displays the caterer's business name, location, cuisine types, price range, and their rating.

Let me click on one of these caterers to see their details."

---

## Guest Caterer Detail (1.5 minutes)
"This is the caterer detail page, accessible to anyone without logging in. We can see the full navbar at the top, the caterer's profile with their business name, location, and rating.

Here's their description, cuisine specialties, and price range per head. Below are their menu packages with descriptions and prices.

There's also a gallery section showing photos of their work, and customer reviews at the bottom.

Notice the Save and Book Now buttons - if I try to click these as a guest, I'll be prompted to sign in first. Let me go back and browse all caterers."

---

## Browse Caterers (1 minute)
"On the browse page, we can see all approved caterers. There's a search bar to find caterers by name or business, and filters for cuisine type, price range, and location.

Let me filter by Filipino cuisine... and you can see the results update instantly. I can also sort by rating or price. Each card shows key information at a glance.

Now let me register as a new client."

---

## Registration (1.5 minutes)
"Here's the registration form. I'll enter my name, email, and phone number. Notice the phone field automatically shows +63 for Philippine numbers - I just need to enter 10 digits starting with 9.

I'll create a password and confirm it. After clicking Register, I'm automatically logged in and redirected to my dashboard."

---

## Client Dashboard (2 minutes)
"Welcome to the client dashboard. The sidebar shows all available sections - Dashboard, Browse Caterers, My Bookings, Saved Caterers, Messages, and Profile Settings.

The dashboard displays my booking statistics: total bookings, pending requests, confirmed events, and completed events.

Below are my recent bookings showing the caterer, event date, status, and total amount. I can click View Details to see more information.

There's also a quick actions section with shortcuts to browse caterers or view all bookings. Let me browse caterers now."

---

## Authenticated Browse (1 minute)
"Now that I'm logged in, the browse page looks similar but notice the navbar has changed - instead of Sign In, I have my profile dropdown with options for Dashboard, Profile Settings, and Logout.

The Save button on each card now works. Let me save this caterer... done! I can access my saved caterers anytime from the sidebar. Let me view a caterer's full details."

---

## Authenticated Caterer Detail (2 minutes)
"The caterer detail page for logged-in users has a simpler navbar with just the logo and my profile dropdown. This keeps the focus on the caterer's information.

Now I can actually save this caterer and book their services. Let me click Book Now.

Here's the booking form. I'll select my event type - let's say Wedding. I'll choose a date, time, venue, and number of guests.

I can select a menu package or leave it blank to discuss custom options. I'll add some special requests in the notes field.

After submitting, my booking request is sent to the caterer. They'll review it and either accept or decline. Let me check my bookings."

---

## My Bookings (1.5 minutes)
"This is my bookings page showing all my booking requests. I can filter by status - All, Pending, Confirmed, Completed, or Cancelled.

Each booking shows the caterer, event details, status with color-coded badges, and the total amount.

Let me view the details of this confirmed booking. Here I can see all the information including the caterer's contact details, event specifics, selected package, and my special requests.

There's a Message Caterer button to communicate directly with them. I can also cancel the booking if needed, though confirmed bookings may have cancellation policies."

---

## Saved Caterers (45 seconds)
"Here are all the caterers I've saved for future reference. Each card shows their key information, and I can quickly unsave them or view their full profile.

This is useful when comparing multiple caterers before making a booking decision. Let me check my messages."

---

## Messages (1.5 minutes)
"The messaging system lets me communicate with caterers. On the left is my conversation list showing all caterers I've messaged.

When I select a conversation, I can see our full message history on the right. The interface is clean and simple - just type my message and send.

This is perfect for discussing menu customizations, asking questions about services, or coordinating event details. Let me check my reviews."

---

## Reviews (1 minute)
"After an event is completed, I can leave a review. Here are all my past reviews.

Let me add a new review for this completed booking. I'll rate the caterer from 1 to 5 stars and write my feedback about their service, food quality, and professionalism.

Once submitted, my review appears on the caterer's detail page, helping other clients make informed decisions. Now let me update my profile."

---

## Profile Settings (1.5 minutes)
"In profile settings, I can update my personal information - name, email, and phone number. The phone field maintains the +63 format.

I can also change my password by entering my current password and the new one.

There's a profile picture upload option to personalize my account. After making changes, I click Update Profile and my information is saved.

Let me show you the How It Works page."

---

## How It Works (45 seconds)
"This page explains the platform to new users. It outlines the simple process: browse caterers, send booking requests, communicate through messages, and leave reviews after events.

It's a helpful resource for first-time users to understand how the platform works. Let me show you the navigation."

---

## Navigation Demo (30 seconds)
"Notice how smooth the navigation is. I can access any section from the sidebar, and the active page is highlighted.

The profile dropdown in the navbar gives quick access to my dashboard and settings. The back button works naturally throughout the site without any redirect issues."

---

## Logout (30 seconds)
"Finally, when I'm done, I can logout from the profile dropdown. This clears my session and redirects me back to the landing page as a guest.

The system prevents any caching issues, so I won't accidentally see authenticated pages after logging out."

---

## Conclusion (30 seconds)
"That's the complete client-side experience! From discovering caterers as a guest, to registering, booking services, communicating with caterers, and managing your events - everything is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Thank you for watching!"

---

**Total Time: ~18 minutes**

**Tips:**
- Speak naturally and at a moderate pace
- Pause briefly when clicking or navigating
- Point out key features as you interact with them
- Keep energy consistent throughout
- Have test data ready (caterers, bookings, messages)
