# Caterer-Side Demo Talk Script

## Introduction (30 seconds)
"Hello! In this video, I'll be demonstrating the caterer-side features of our catering booking platform. This is where caterers manage their business profile, handle booking requests, communicate with clients, and grow their catering business. Let's get started."

---

## Landing Page & Registration (1 minute)
"Starting from the landing page, I'll click on 'For Caterers' in the navigation menu.

This takes me to the caterer registration page. Here I'll enter my business information - business name, owner name, email, and phone number. The phone field uses Philippine format with +63, and I just enter 10 digits starting with 9.

I'll create a secure password and click Register. Once registered, my account is created but needs admin approval before I can appear on the platform. Let me login with an approved caterer account."

---

## Caterer Dashboard (2 minutes)
"Welcome to the caterer dashboard! The sidebar shows all my sections - Dashboard, My Profile, Bookings, Menu Packages, Messages, and Reviews.

The dashboard gives me a complete overview of my business performance. At the top, I can see key metrics: total bookings received, pending requests waiting for my response, confirmed upcoming events, and my average rating from client reviews.

Below that is my revenue summary showing total revenue from all bookings and revenue from completed events only.

The recent bookings section displays my latest booking requests with client names, event dates, status, and amounts. I can quickly view details or respond to requests right from here.

There's also a quick actions panel with shortcuts to update my profile, manage packages, or view all bookings. Let me check my profile first."

---

## Caterer Profile (2.5 minutes)
"This is my business profile page. At the top, I can see my current profile picture, business name, and approval status. Since I'm approved, I'm visible to clients on the platform.

Here's all my business information: business name, owner name, contact email, phone number, and location. I can edit any of these by clicking Edit Profile.

Below is my business description where I tell clients about my catering services, experience, and what makes my business special.

The cuisine specialties section shows what types of cuisine I offer - Filipino, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, etc. I can add or remove specialties based on my menu.

My price range per head is displayed here - this gives clients an idea of my pricing. I set a minimum and maximum price.

There's also a gallery section where I can upload photos of my dishes, events, and setups. High-quality photos help attract more clients.

At the bottom, I can see all the reviews clients have left for me. These reviews build trust and credibility. Let me update my profile."

---

## Edit Profile (2 minutes)
"In the edit profile form, I can update my business information. Let me change my business description to highlight my specialties better.

I can update my location, phone number, and email if needed. The phone field maintains the +63 format.

For cuisine specialties, I'll add a few more options - let me add Korean and Mediterranean to expand my offerings.

I'll adjust my price range per head. Let's say my minimum is 350 pesos and maximum is 800 pesos per head.

For the gallery, I can upload new photos. Let me add some images of recent events I've catered. I can also remove old photos if needed.

After making all changes, I click Update Profile and everything is saved. Now let me manage my menu packages."

---

## Menu Packages (2 minutes)
"This is where I create and manage my menu packages. I currently have several packages displayed here - each showing the package name, description, price, and action buttons.

Let me create a new package. I'll click Add New Package.

I'll name it 'Premium Wedding Package', add a detailed description of what's included - appetizers, main courses, desserts, and beverages.

I'll set the price at 650 pesos per head. This package is perfect for elegant wedding receptions.

After clicking Create, my new package is added and will be visible to clients viewing my profile.

I can also edit existing packages if I need to update prices or descriptions, or delete packages that I no longer offer. Let me check my bookings now."

---

## Bookings Management (3 minutes)
"Here's my bookings page showing all booking requests from clients. I can filter by status - All, Pending, Confirmed, Completed, or Cancelled.

Let me filter by Pending to see requests that need my attention. These are new booking inquiries waiting for my response.

Each booking shows the client's name, event type, date, number of guests, and the estimated total. Let me view the details of this pending request.

Here I can see complete information: the client's contact details, event type, date, time, venue, number of guests, selected menu package if any, and any special requests they've included.

The estimated total is calculated based on guests and my average price per head, or the package price if they selected one.

Now I have two options: Accept or Decline. If I accept, the booking status changes to Confirmed and the client is notified. If I decline, I should provide a reason.

Let me accept this booking. I'll click Accept Booking... and it's confirmed! The client will receive a notification and can now see this as a confirmed event.

For confirmed bookings, I can view all details and communicate with the client through messages. Once the event is completed, the status will change to Completed and the client can leave a review.

Let me check a completed booking. Here I can see the final details and the review the client left for me. This helps build my reputation on the platform."

---

## Messages (1.5 minutes)
"The messaging system lets me communicate directly with my clients. On the left is my conversation list showing all clients I'm chatting with.

When I select a conversation, I can see our complete message history. This is where clients ask questions about menu customizations, dietary restrictions, or event logistics.

Let me respond to this client's question about vegetarian options. I'll type my message explaining the vegetarian dishes I can prepare... and send.

The interface is simple and efficient - perfect for quick communication. Clients can also initiate conversations from my profile page or booking details. Let me check my reviews."

---

## Reviews & Ratings (1.5 minutes)
"This page shows all the reviews clients have left for me after their events. Each review displays the client's name, their rating from 1 to 5 stars, their written feedback, and the date.

These reviews are crucial for my business - they appear on my public profile and help potential clients make decisions.

I can see my overall average rating at the top, which is also displayed on my profile card when clients browse caterers.

Positive reviews help me get featured on the landing page and attract more bookings. I can read through all feedback to understand what clients appreciate and where I can improve.

Let me go back to my dashboard to see the complete overview."

---

## Dashboard Overview (1 minute)
"Back on the dashboard, I can monitor my business performance at a glance. The statistics update in real-time as I receive new bookings and complete events.

I can see my pending requests that need immediate attention, track my confirmed upcoming events, and monitor my revenue growth.

The recent bookings section keeps me updated on the latest activity, and the quick actions panel gives me fast access to important tasks.

This dashboard is my command center for managing my catering business on the platform. Let me show you the navigation."

---

## Navigation Demo (30 seconds)
"Notice how easy it is to navigate between sections. The sidebar clearly shows all available options, and the active page is highlighted.

The profile dropdown in the navbar gives quick access to my dashboard and logout option.

Everything is organized logically - profile management, booking handling, package creation, client communication, and review monitoring all in dedicated sections."

---

## Logout (30 seconds)
"When I'm done managing my business for the day, I can logout from the profile dropdown. This securely ends my session and redirects me back to the landing page.

The system ensures no caching issues, so my business data remains secure after logout."

---

## Conclusion (45 seconds)
"That's the complete caterer-side experience! From registering your business and creating your profile, to managing menu packages, handling booking requests, communicating with clients, and building your reputation through reviews.

The platform provides all the tools you need to run your catering business efficiently and professionally. You can focus on what you do best - creating amazing food experiences - while we handle the booking management.

Thank you for watching!"

---

**Total Time: ~18 minutes**

**Tips:**
- Speak naturally and confidently
- Emphasize business benefits (revenue tracking, client management)
- Pause when clicking or navigating
- Show enthusiasm when discussing features
- Have test data ready (bookings, packages, messages, reviews)
- Highlight how features help grow their business
